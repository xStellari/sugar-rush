Ordering
You can place an order through our order form on this website, or message us directly on Facebook or Instagram. We'll get back to you to confirm your order and send payment details.
We require at least 24 hours notice for all orders since every cup is handcrafted fresh to order. For bulk orders of 6 or more cups, we recommend ordering 2–3 days in advance to make sure we can accommodate you.

Pricing & Payment

Pickup & Delivery
We're based in Davao City. The exact pickup location will be confirmed when we reach out after you place your order.
Delivery is not available at the moment — orders are pickup only. We're working on making this available in the future. Follow us on social media to stay updated.
The cups are best enjoyed immediately after pickup for the best texture and flavor. Toppings may soften over time. If storing, keep them refrigerated and consume within the same day.
Products
We currently have three flavors in our Cake in a Cup series: Turon Banoffee, Polvoron Delight, and Halo-Halo Inspired. Visit our menu page for the full ingredient breakdown of each flavor.

The Sugar Rush Cake Station is an interactive dessert rental service for events. We set up a fully styled station where your guests can build their own cake-in-a-cup from scratch — choosing their cake base, icing, toppings, and syrup. Learn more on our Cake Station page.
Cake Station packages are still being finalized. In the meantime, you can express your interest by messaging us on Facebook or Instagram, or drop your email on the Cake Station page to be notified when bookings open.
