A Sweet Idea Born

Sugar Rush transforms classic Filipino flavors into modern, portable cake-in-a-cup creations. We saw a gap in the market — repetitive dessert options, limited innovation, and a lack of convenience.

Every cup is layered with love, handcrafted fresh, and designed to give you that perfect sweet rush wherever you are.
