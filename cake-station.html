What is the Cake Station?

Mix & Munch Your Way

The Sugar Rush Cake Station is a fully styled, interactive dessert setup designed for events — birthdays, debuts, team parties, reunions, and more. Guests get to build their own cake-in-a-cup from scratch, choosing their base, icing, syrup, and toppings.

We bring the station to you: setup, styling, all the ingredients, and everything your guests need for a one-of-a-kind dessert experience. No baking required on your end.
